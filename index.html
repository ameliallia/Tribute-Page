Frankie Knuckles

The Godfather of House Music

Musical Career

  • Born in The Bronx, Los Angeles (January 18, 1955).
  • While studying textile design, worked as DJ.
  • Played soul, disco, and R&B at two of the most important discos.
  • In 1970's moved to Chicago. WHere a old friend was opening the nightclub called Warehouse.
  • In 1982 he started his own club The Power Plant.
